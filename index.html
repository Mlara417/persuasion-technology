Digital Marketing through Online Platforms and Artificial Intelligence

Data is constantly being collected about you. Using a search browser such as Google, or a social media platform such as Facebook makes you a target of persuasive technologies. This is all in exchange for using their service. Digital Marketing uses Artificial Intelligence to try and control your actions and behavior.

This has been consistent through the years with newspapers, advertisements, and commercials. All aimed at getting the reader to become interested in a product. Digital Marketing is a sales technique aimed at your core as an individual based on information these platforms collect on you.

The best way to defend yourself is to educate yourself.

What can you do?

Install Ad-Blocker

Find an ad blocker extension that works with your browser. Find something that blocks items such as banners, pop-up ads, and tracking scripts.

Limit Time Online

As we spend more time online we provide more data to the algorithms built to adjust our behavior. Spend some time doing something for yourself in order to give yourself a break. The key here is moderation.

Eliminate Social Media

Social media platforms are ripe with tools meant to collect your data. From forms, to adverstisements. Eliminating this from your life will help attain an inner perspective without the outside noise constantly distracting you.

Switch to Private Browser

Switch to a browser that has privacy as it's main focus. Some browsers that are more security focused are Opera and Brave. They both use the Chromium rendering engine meaning it should be a familiar experience to Google Chrome.
